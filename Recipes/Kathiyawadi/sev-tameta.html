Sev Tamatar

Sev Tamatar is a popular Gujarati dish made with a tangy and spicy tomato curry, topped with crispy sev (fried chickpea flour noodles). It's a flavorful, quick-to-make snack or light meal, often enjoyed with bread or khakra. The dish combines the richness of tomatoes with the crunch of sev for a delightful contrast.

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time
25 minutes

Servings: 2

Cuisine: Gujarati
