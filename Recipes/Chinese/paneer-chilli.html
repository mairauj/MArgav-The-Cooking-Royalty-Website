Paneer Chilli

Paneer Chilli is a vibrant and flavorful Indo-Chinese dish that combines the richness of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) with a spicy and tangy sauce. This dish features crisp paneer cubes, lightly fried to perfection, and tossed with bell peppers, onions, and a medley of aromatic spices.

Prep Time: 08-09 minutes
Cook Time: 10-12 minutes

Total Time
18-21 minutes

Servings: 2-4

Cuisine: Indian

    • For Chilli Tempayy
