Paneer Makhni Pizza

Paneer Makhni Pizza is a fusion dish that combines the rich, creamy flavors of Indian paneer makhni with the classic Italian pizza. The pizza is topped with a velvety makhni sauce, cubes of paneer, and a blend of melted cheese, creating a deliciously indulgent combination of spices and cheese. It’s a unique twist on traditional pizza, offering a perfect balance of creamy, spicy, and cheesy goodness.

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20-30 minutes

Total Time
35-40 minutes

Servings: 2

Cuisine: Indian

✦Ingredients ➦Instructions
  • For Makhani Gravy
  • 1 ½ tbsp Oil
  • ½ tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • ¼ inch Cinnamon stick
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • ½ inch Ginger, slice
  • 2 medium size Onions, cut into half
  • 7 small size Tomatoes, cut into Cubed
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 ½ tsp Degi red chili powder
  • ¼ tsp Asafoetida (optional)
  • ⅓ cup Water
  • 8-9 Cashewnut
  • ½ tsp Sugar
  • For Dough
  • 1 cup Refined flour
  • 2 tbsp Semolina
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Curd, beaten
  • Water as required
  • 1 tsp Ghee
  • ½ tsp Baking soda
  • For Stuffing
  • ½ cup Pizza cheese blend
  • 2 Green chillies (less spicy chopped)
  • 1 ½ tbsp Coriander leaves, chopped
  • For Paneer Marination
  • 200 gms Paneer, cut into small cubes
  • 1 medium size Onion, cut into cube
  • 1 medium size Yellow bell pepper, cut into cube
  • ½ Green bell pepper, cut into cube
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Oil
  • ½ tsp Degi red chili powder
  • A pinch of coriander powder
  • A pinch of asafoetida
  • 1 tbsp Coriander leaves, chopped
  • For Tossing
  • 1 tsp Oil
  • Marinated Paneer Mixture
  • ¼ tsp Dry fenugreek leaves, crushed
  • For Makhani Sauce
  • 2-3 tbsp Butter, cubed
  • 1 ½ heaped tsp Degi red chilli powder
  • ½ tsp Coriander powder
  • A pinch of asafoetida
  • Prepared Makhani Gravy
  • ¼ cup Water
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ tsp Sugar
  • 2 tbsp fresh Cream
  • For Assembling
  • Prepared Pizza Base
  • Prepared Makhani Sauce
  • Paneer Mixture
  • Pizza cheese blend
  • Prepared Makhani Sauce
  • For Garnish
  • Coriander sprig
    • For Makhani Gravy
    1. Firstly, in a saucepan add oil, once it gets hot add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon stick and let it splutter well.
    2. Add ginger, garlic, onions and saute on medium flame. Add tomatoes, salt to taste. Saute it for 10-15 seconds.
    3. Now, add degi red chili powder, asafoetida, water, cashew nuts and a pinch of sugar.
    4. Let it simmer for 15-20 minutes. Cool it completely.
    5. Transfer it into a grinder and grind it into a smooth paste.
    6. Keep it aside for further use.
    • For Dough
    1. In a parat or bowl, add refined flour, semolina, salt to taste, curd and water.
    2. Knead it into loose dough. Now in a small bowl, add ghee and baking soda and mix well.
    3. Add this mixture into the dough and knead it properly, cover it with a muslin cloth and keep aside for further use.
    4. Let it rest for at least 15-20 minutes.
    • For Stuffing
    1. In a bowl add pizza cheese blend, green chillies and coriander leaves.
    2. Mix it well and keep aside for further use.
    • For Paneer Marination
    1. In a bowl, add paneer, onion, yellow bell pepper, green bell pepper, salt to taste, oil, degi red chili powder, coriander powder, asafoetida and coriander leaves.
    2. Mix it well and keep aside for further use.
    • For Tossing
    1. In a saucepan, add oil, once it gets hot add marinated paneer mixture and kasoori methi.
    2. Toss it well for 20-30 seconds. Keep it aside for further use.
    • For Makhani Sauce
    1. In a saucepan, add butter, let it melt. Add degi red chili powder, coriander powder and asafoetida and saute well.
    2. Add prepared Makhani gravy, little water and stir well.
    3. Now, add salt to taste, sugar, fresh cream and mix well. Give it one boil and switch off the flame.
    • For Pizza Base
    1. First, apply ghee on both palms. Now, take one portion of dough and add prepared cheese stuffing in the center and shape it into a round ball.
    2. Roll it thin using a rolling pin.
    • For Assembling
    1. Firstly, dust some semolina and take the prepared pizza base and roll it thin using a rolling pin.
    2. Now, cook it on a flat pan slightly crisp from one side. Flip it and cook from the other side as well. Apply prepared makhani sauce on one side.
    3. Add prepared paneer mixture, pizza cheese blend and some more makhani sauce.
    4. Cover it with a lid and cook on medium flame until the cheese completely melts.
    5. Garnish it with coriander sprig and serve hot.
