Sandwich

A sandwich is a versatile meal consisting of layers of fillings, such as cheeses, vegetables, and spreads, sandwiched between slices of bread. It can be customized to suit any taste, making it perfect for quick lunches or picnics

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time
25 minutes

Servings: 2

Cuisine: Indian

    • For Potato Masala
    1. Heat oil in a nonstick pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves let it splutter.
    2. Add boiled potato and mash well.
    3. Add asafoetida, turmeric powder and salt to taste and mix everything well and cook for a few minutes.
    4. Add chopped green chilli, coriander leaves and green peas, mix well and cook for more 2 minutes.
    5. Once it‚Äôs done, transfer it into a bowl.
    • For Green chutney
    1. In a grinder jar, add ginger, green chilli, coriander leaves, mint leaves, ice cube and water as required grind it into a smooth paste.
    2. Transfer it into a bowl, add salt to taste, chaat masala and mix well.
    • For Sandwich
    1. Spread butter evenly on the bread slices.
    2. Now spread the coriander chutney.
    3. Spread the potato masala filling on the bread slices.
    4. Place the slices of onion, tomato, capsicum, beetroot.
    5. Sprinkle a pinch of chaat masala, salt to taste and roasted cumin powder.
    6. Place the cheese slice on top.
    7. Cover with the slices on which we have spread the butter and chutney.
    8. In a preheated grill pan. Grease it with some butter and place the sandwiches.
    9. Toast the sandwiches from both sides till crisp and golden.
    10. Remove from the grill pan and spread some green chutney, grated cheese, sev on top of the sandwiches.
    11. Cut into halves and Serve with tomato ketchup and coriander chutney.
